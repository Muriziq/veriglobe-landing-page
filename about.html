About Veriglobe

Veriglobe is a bold identity verification platform proudly built by two teenagers. We're not backed by a huge corporation or VC money — just passion, code, and a mission to change the game.

Veriglobe was founded in 2023, and by August 2024, we launched our platform as our first step toward building a safer internet. Our goal is simple: make identity verification fast, affordable, and accessible — especially for startups and businesses that can't afford overpriced solutions.

We're building this from the ground up — no fluff, just real tools that work. From API-first development to simple pricing and a dashboard that makes sense, every part of Veriglobe reflects the way we believe modern tech should be: easy to use, fairly priced, and always improving.

This is just the beginning. We may be young, but we're here to stay — and we're not afraid to challenge the status quo.
